## Snow pants — features (all pants)

- 15k waterproof rating on ALL pants.
- Two interior vents.
- Lots of pockets, belt loops, and a drawstring waist.
- Double layer at the bottom around the ankle for durability.
- Gaiters: a double layer around the ankle that goes over your boots to keep snow out.

## Snow pants — colorways & fabric

All are 15k waterproof; the fabric weight differs by colorway:
- **Lightweight & breathable:** black, light purp, light pink, black and white, gray, olive green.
- **Heavyweight (more durable):** murdered out, pink camo, jwhites, pow purps, maroons.
- **Heavier still:** jags, corduroys.
- **Denims and cords:** have a waterproof inner layer and are waterproof treated on the outside too.
- **Murdered out:** all black (black embroidery, zippers, and stitching), a lowkey look, made
  with a more durable cordura fabric so it's extra waterproof. It's the pair the owner rides.

## Sizing

The pants **run big**. Sizing is by HEIGHT and all sizes fit all waists. Tell people to
**size down or go true to size** (true to size = baggy, size down = a bit less baggy). The
size chart also lives in the Instagram highlights. Ask their height if they haven't said.

## Care / washing

Machine wash in warm water, tumble dry on low heat.

- Shipping cost is calculated by location and items, and shown before purchase. They aim
  for the cheapest and fastest rates.
- International duties/taxes are not included.
